# Welcome email — first login

A ready-to-send welcome that goes to each person the first time they sign in.
It's written to work for **any department** (the Guide inside the CRM handles the
department-specific detail). Placeholders in {curly braces} get filled in per
person. Plain, warm, and short — people actually read short.

---

**Subject:** Welcome to Ameya Heights CRM, {FirstName} 👋

**Body:**

Hi {FirstName},

Welcome aboard — your Ameya Heights CRM account is ready. This is the one place
your day lives: your leads, tasks, payments, documents, site work and messages,
all together.

**Three things to do first (about 5 minutes):**

1. **Sign in and set up your profile.** {LoginURL} → top-right → Settings. Add a
   photo and set your password (at least 8 characters).
2. **Make it comfortable.** Top bar → the sliders icon → tap **“Easy view”** for
   roomy spacing and larger text. You can fine-tune it any time; it’s remembered
   on your device.
3. **Open your Guide.** It’s the first thing on your Home page and in the menu
   (**Guide**). It walks you through your first steps and every feature, and you
   can search it whenever you’re unsure.

**A couple of shortcuts worth knowing on day one:**

- Press **⌘K** (Ctrl+K on Windows) anywhere to jump to any screen or find any
  lead, task, buyer or file.
- Start each morning on **Today’s Priorities** and the **Home** dashboard —
  everything due, plus your key numbers, in one glance.
- The **bell** (top bar) tells you what needs you; **Messages** lets you chat
  anyone on the team by @username.

Your role decides what you see, so your menu already shows the areas that are
yours. If something looks missing or you’d like access to more, just reply to
this email or ask your manager.

Welcome again — we’re glad you’re here.

The Ameya Heights team
