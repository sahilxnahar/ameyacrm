# User Guide

Welcome to the Ameya Heights CRM — your daily workspace.

## Signing in

Go to the app URL, enter your **username or email** and password. If your account has
two‑factor enabled, enter the 6‑digit code from your authenticator app (or a backup code).
You can **trust a device** for 30 days to skip 2FA on that device.

First login asks you to set a new password (≥12 characters, with upper/lower case, a number
and a symbol).

## Getting around

- **Sidebar** — your modules (only what you have access to).
- **⌘K / Ctrl‑K** — command palette to jump anywhere instantly.
- **Search** — top bar searches tasks, leads, documents, people and requests.
- **Bell** — notifications; **Mark all read** clears the badge.
- **Theme** — light/dark toggle. Install the app (see below) for a native feel.

## Dashboard

Your personalized home: work assigned to you, what’s **due today**, **upcoming** deadlines,
items **awaiting your approval**, recent documents and department updates.

## Tasks

- **Board** (Kanban) — drag cards between columns to change status. **List** — sortable table.
- **New task** — title, description, priority, department/project, due date, estimate and
  assignees.
- **Open a task** to see details. If it’s assigned to you, you can **Accept**, **Reject**
  (with a reason), **Request clarification**, update **progress**, or **Mark complete**.
- Use the **checklist** for sub‑steps and milestones, and **comments** (type `@username` to
  mention someone — they’ll be notified).

## Sales & Leads

Pipeline board by stage (New → … → Won/Lost). **New lead** captures contact, source, budget,
requirement and owner; tick **NRI** to add country/time‑zone. Drag a lead to move its stage.
Open a lead for its full **communication timeline**.

## Documents

Browse **folders**, open subfolders via breadcrumbs. **Upload** into a folder (files are
versioned automatically). Download with the download icon (downloads are permission‑checked
and logged). Documents can carry **expiry** dates for licences/approvals.

## Billing

Tabs for **Invoices**, **Purchase Orders**, **Vendor Bills**. Create an invoice with line
items; GST (CGST+SGST intra‑state, or IGST) and totals are computed for you.

## Material Requests

**New request** to order materials (Cement, Steel, Tiles, …). Add line items, pick a
recipient and approvers, set priority and needed‑by date. The system **generates a
structured email** and routes the request for **approval**. Approvers act right from the
list.

## Calendar

See upcoming meetings, site visits, deadlines and milestones. **Add to my calendar (ICS)**
exports events you can import into Google/Apple/Outlook.

## Notifications

You’re notified for new assignments, status changes, comments/mentions, approvals,
deadlines, meetings, document and material‑request updates, and announcements. Manage
channels and quiet hours in your preferences.

## Install as an app

- **Android/desktop (Chrome/Edge):** you’ll see an **Install** prompt, or use the browser
  menu → *Install app*.
- **iPhone/iPad (Safari):** Share → **Add to Home Screen**.

Installed, it opens full‑screen, works offline for recently viewed pages, and can receive
push notifications where supported.

## Account & security

**Settings → Security:** change your password, enable/disable **2FA**, view **active
sessions** and **recent login activity**. If something looks unfamiliar, tell your admin.
